COMPANY OVERVIEW

Fluid Investment Group LLC is a registered government contracting entity specializing in project management, consulting, and subcontracting support services. Based in Ocala, Florida, we provide efficient, reliable, and high-quality solutions across a wide range of projects for both public and private sector partners nationwide.

Our team brings professional expertise in facilities management, technical services, and administrative support to deliver cost-effective solutions that meet and exceed client expectations.
